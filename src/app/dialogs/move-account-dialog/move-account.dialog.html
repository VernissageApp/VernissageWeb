Move account

This action will move your account to another account in the Fediverse, such as another Vernissage, Mastodon, Pixelfed, or compatible ActivityPub server.

After the migration is started, your followers will be notified about your new account. If their servers support account migration, they may automatically unfollow this account and follow your new account instead.

Please read the following information carefully before continuing:

  • Only your followers will be moved automatically.
  • Your posts, photos, comments, likes, bookmarks, followed accounts, blocks, mutes, and other account data will not be transferred.
  • Your current profile will be marked as moved and will display a redirect notice pointing to the new account.
  • Your current account may no longer be fully usable after the migration.
  • The new account must first be configured to reference this account as an alias or previous account.
  • After moving, there may be a waiting period before you can move again.
  • You should export your data before continuing, especially if you want to keep a copy of your content or import followed accounts elsewhere.
